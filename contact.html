Reason for the site

The main reason for the site was i wanted a place to track my workout stats, volume lifted / current max weight etc. Then i decided to take it one step further and build a node backend server to it where i could track my workouts live as i was doing them, it shows me what i should be lifting and automatically adds weight every set couple of weeks, it's like having a virtual coach. I'm going to do a post on the setup for this entire site at some point so keep an eye out for that.

Another reason is being a web developer i should really have some kind of online presence, even if it's just a bunch of random info that nobody really cares about but me. The site will have blog posts and about some of the things i work on outside and during work.
